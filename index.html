Privacy Policy for QuickBill

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

QuickBill respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the QuickBill mobile application.

1. Information You Enter into QuickBill

QuickBill allows you to record expenses, notes, categories, dates, and other information related to your personal bookkeeping.

Your bookkeeping records are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud synchronization, your records are also stored in your personal Apple iCloud account so that they can remain synchronized across your devices.

2. iCloud Synchronization

QuickBill uses Apple's iCloud service to provide synchronization between devices signed in to your iCloud account.

Your synchronized records are stored in your personal iCloud account. QuickBill does not operate a separate server for storing your bookkeeping records.

Your use of iCloud is subject to Apple's applicable terms and privacy practices.

3. Developer Access to Your Records

QuickBill does not use your bookkeeping records for advertising, marketing, user profiling, or tracking.

The developer does not sell, rent, or disclose your bookkeeping records to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

QuickBill does not use third-party advertising services or tracking technologies.

QuickBill uses Apple's iCloud service solely to provide synchronization functionality.

5. Data Storage and Deletion

You may delete individual records within QuickBill.

Records stored locally may be removed when you delete them within the app or uninstall the app, subject to your device and backup settings.

Records synchronized through iCloud may remain available through your iCloud account until they are deleted from the app or otherwise removed through your iCloud settings.

6. Children's Privacy

QuickBill is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact
